## 🎯 Current Work

### 🛡️ [**Sentinel-Net**](https://github.com/Mroziy/Sentinel-Net)
**AI-Powered WAF + Network Anomaly Detector**

An open-source cybersecurity platform protecting organizations from sophisticated cyberattacks (DDoS, SQLi, XSS, zero-day intrusions). Features:
- **Regex-Based Reverse Proxy WAF:** Blocks OWASP Top 10 attacks at the edge
- **AI Anomaly Detection:** Isolation Forest (unsupervised learning) flags zero-day threats in real-time
- **Self-Hosted & Cloud-Agnostic:** Digital sovereignty — your data never leaves your infrastructure
- **Free & Accessible:** MIT Licensed for activists, journalists, and digital rights defenders

**Status:** Open-sourced for the Open Technology Fund (OTF) and Presidential AI Competition
**Tech Stack:** Python, scikit-learn, FastAPI, Docker
