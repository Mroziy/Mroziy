## 🎯 Current Work

### 🛡️ [**Sentinel-Net**](https://github.com/Mroziy/Sentinel-Net)
**AI-Powered WAF + Network Anomaly Detector**

An open-source, self-hosted cybersecurity platform protecting NGOs, journalists, and at-risk organizations from DDoS, SQLi, XSS, and zero-day intrusions — with no cloud dependency.

- **Regex-Based Reverse Proxy WAF:** Blocks OWASP Top 10 attacks at the edge
- **AI Anomaly Detection:** Isolation Forest (unsupervised ML) flags zero-day threats in real-time
- **Self-Hosted & Cloud-Agnostic:** Your data never leaves your infrastructure
- **MIT Licensed:** Completely free for activists, journalists, and digital rights defenders

**Status:** Open-sourced for the Open Technology Fund (OTF) and Presidential AI Competition  
**Tech Stack:** Python · scikit-learn · FastAPI · Docker
